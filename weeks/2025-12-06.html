Ingredients
  • 3.5 oz eggs
  • 1/4 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1 oz feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 2 oz whole-grain bread
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat olive oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  • Sauté peppers and mushrooms 3 minutes, then add spinach until wilted.
  • Pour beaten eggs over veggies, season, and cook 2–3 minutes.
  • Fold in feta and cook 1 minute more.
  • Toast bread and serve on the side.

  • Drizzle maple syrup and serve warm.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed greens
  • 2 Tbsp chimichurri
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe

  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper

Prep Notes / Recipe
