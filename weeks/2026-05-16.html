Focus: Gut Health & Fiber

This week's meals are built around Gut Health & Fiber. Plates feature fermented foods (kefir, sauerkraut, kimchi, miso), prebiotic fibers (oats, legumes, alliums, root veg), and a wide rotation of plants to feed a diverse microbiome. Each meal is whole-food, home-cook-friendly, and ready in 40 minutes or less.

Ingredients
  • 0.5 cup rolled oats
  • 0.75 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 Tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 0.5 cup mixed berries
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup
  • 0.25 tsp cinnamon
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Combine oats, almond milk, flax, maple syrup, and cinnamon in a jar
  • Stir well and seal with a lid
  • Refrigerate overnight (at least 6 hours)
  • Top with berries before serving

Ingredients

Ingredients
