Focus: Blood Sugar Balance

This week focuses on balanced plates: protein + fiber + healthy fats with slow-digesting carbs for steady energy. Meals favor berries, beans, oats, quinoa, and veggies while keeping added sugars low.

  • Build meals: protein + fiber + fat.
  • Choose low-GI carbs (oats, beans, quinoa).
  • Walk 10 minutes after bigger meals.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 3 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 Tbsp chopped walnuts
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Whisk almond milk, chia, vanilla, and cinnamon.
  • Refrigerate 20–30 minutes until thick.
  • Stir to break clumps.
  • Top with blueberries and walnuts to serve.

Ingredients
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp crumbled feta
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat oil in skillet over medium.
  • Sauté mushrooms 3 minutes, add spinach to wilt.
  • Whisk eggs with salt and pour over veggies.
  • Cook, fold with feta, and serve.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds

  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon

Prep Notes / Recipe
