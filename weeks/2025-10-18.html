Breakfasts for the Week

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • Cook oats according to package.
  • Top with berries and honey.

Lunches for the Week

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes
  • Grill chicken until cooked.
  • Toss with greens and tomatoes.

Dinners for the Week

  • 1 salmon fillet
  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 1 lemon
  • Preheat oven to 400°F.
  • Bake salmon and asparagus for 20 mins.
  • Squeeze lemon over before serving.

Snacks for the Week

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • Mix honey into yogurt.
