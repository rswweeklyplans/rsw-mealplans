Focus: Micronutrient Density

This week's meals are built around Micronutrient Density. Each plate is designed to be whole-food, home-cook-friendly, and easy to prepare in 40 minutes or less.

Ingredients
  • 3 large organic eggs
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup sliced cremini mushrooms
  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • 1 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat olive oil in skillet over medium heat
  • Sauté mushrooms for 4 minutes until golden
  • Add spinach and cook until wilted about 2 minutes
  • Whisk eggs with salt and pepper then pour into skillet
  • Scramble until just set about 3 minutes
  • Serve topped with sliced avocado
