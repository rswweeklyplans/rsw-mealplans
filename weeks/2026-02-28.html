Focus: Protein Prioritization

This week emphasizes steady, satisfying protein at each meal to support energy, recovery, and craving control. We pair lean proteins with fiber-rich sides and simple sauces so prep stays quick and repeatable.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1 oz feta crumbles
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat olive oil in a nonstick skillet over medium.
  • Sauté onion and bell pepper for 3–4 minutes.
  • Pour in egg whites and cook, stirring, until set.
  • Fold in spinach and feta to wilt and warm.
  • Season with salt and pepper and serve.

Ingredients
  • 8 oz flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups mixed stir-fry vegetables
  • 2 cups cooked brown rice
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Whisk soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, and 2 Tbsp water.
  • Heat olive and 1 tsp sesame oil in a skillet; sauté garlic and ginger 30 seconds.
  • Add beef and stir-fry 2–3 minutes.
  • Add vegetables and cook 3–4 minutes until crisp-tender.
  • Pour in sauce and toss 1–2 minutes to thicken.
  • Serve over warm brown rice and drizzle remaining sesame oil.

Ingredients
  • 6 oz cod fillet

  • 1 Tbsp white miso paste
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Whisk miso, honey, vinegar, and soy sauce.
  • Brush cod with half the glaze.
  • Sear bok choy in olive oil 2 minutes, add 2 Tbsp water, cover 3 minutes.
  • Pan-sear cod in 1 tsp sesame oil 2 minutes per side, then cover 2–3 minutes.
  • Drizzle remaining glaze and sesame oil over cod and serve with warm rice.

  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried rosemary
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 2 Tbsp milk
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Preheat oven to 425°F.

  • Rest pork 5 minutes, slice, and serve with beans and mash.
