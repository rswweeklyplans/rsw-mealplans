Week start (ISO): 2025-11-15

Focus: Hormone Harmony

This week features crucifers, omega-3s, citrus, and flax to support hormone balance. Meals stay protein-forward with plenty of fiber and healthy fats.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 Tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 1 Tbsp almond butter
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch sea salt
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Bring almond milk and salt to a simmer in a saucepan.
  • Stir in oats and cook 5-7 minutes until creamy.
  • Fold in blueberries, cinnamon, and vanilla.
  • Remove from heat and stir in ground flaxseed.
  • Top with almond butter and serve.

Ingredients
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 Tbsp water
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Whisk eggs with water, salt, and pepper.
  • Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  • Sauté mushrooms 3-4 minutes, then add spinach to wilt.
  • Pour eggs over veggies and cook until just set; fold.
  • Slide onto a plate and top with avocado.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed berries

Ingredients
  • 8 oz turkey sausage, casing removed
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups diced sweet potato
  • 2 cups chopped kale
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat oil in a skillet and brown turkey sausage 4-5 minutes.
  • Add sweet potato and onion; cover and cook 8-10 minutes, stirring.
  • Stir in kale, paprika, and salt; cook 3-4 minutes to wilt.
  • Serve hot.

Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup packed spinach
  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 Tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 1 Tbsp hemp hearts
  • 1 oz plain protein powder
  • 1/2 cup ice
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Add almond milk to blender.
  • Layer in spinach, berries, flax, hemp, and protein powder.
  • Add ice.
  • Blend until smooth 45-60 seconds.
  • Pour into a glass and serve.

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup dry quinoa, rinsed
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup diced ripe pear
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 Tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Simmer quinoa with water in a saucepan 12 minutes.
  • Stir in almond milk, cinnamon, and vanilla.
  • Cook 5-7 minutes until creamy.
  • Fold in diced pear.
  • Serve topped with pumpkin seeds and maple syrup.

Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups canned light coconut milk
  • 1/3 cup chia seeds
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 cup raspberries
  • 1 Tbsp cacao nibs
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Whisk coconut milk, vanilla, and maple syrup in a bowl.
  • Stir in chia seeds until evenly distributed.
  • Refrigerate 20-30 minutes, stirring once.
  • Portion into bowls and top with raspberries.
  • Sprinkle cacao nibs and serve.

Ingredients

Ingredients
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1/2 cup diced carrot
  • 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
  • 4 cups low-sodium vegetable broth
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Sauté onion, celery, and carrot in olive oil 5 minutes.
  • Add ginger and turmeric; cook 30 seconds.
  • Stir in lentils and broth; bring to a simmer.
  • Cook 15-18 minutes until lentils are tender.
  • Season with salt and lemon juice and serve.

Ingredients
  • 4 cups chopped kale
  • 1 cup canned chickpeas, rinsed
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 Tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Massage kale with 1 tsp olive oil for 1 minute.

  • Toss kale with chickpeas and tomatoes.

  • Toss again and serve.

Ingredients
  • 10 oz extra-firm tofu, cubed
  • 2 tsp avocado oil
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell pepper
  • 2 Tbsp coconut aminos
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp grated garlic
  • 1/4 cup cashews
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Pat tofu dry and sear in 1 tsp oil until golden, 5 minutes.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb bone-in chicken thighs
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 2 cups halved Brussels sprouts
  • 2 cups diced sweet potato
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Preheat oven to 425°F.
  • Toss chicken with olive oil, rosemary, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  • Spread Brussels sprouts and sweet potato on a sheet pan.
  • Nestle chicken on the pan.
  • Roast 30-35 minutes until chicken is 165°F and veggies are tender.

Ingredients
  • 12 oz salmon fillets
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 cups cooked brown rice
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Preheat oven to 400°F and line a sheet pan.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 4 cups roasted spaghetti squash strands
  • 2 Tbsp chopped fresh basil
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Mix turkey with almond flour, egg, seasoning, and salt; form 12 meatballs.
  • Sear meatballs in a skillet 5-6 minutes to brown.
  • Pour in marinara and simmer 10 minutes until cooked through.
  • Warm spaghetti squash in a pan or microwave.
  • Serve meatballs and sauce over squash and top with basil.

Ingredients
  • 12 oz cod fillets
  • 1 Tbsp white miso paste
  • 1 Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 2 tsp sesame oil, divided
  • 2 cups sliced shiitake mushrooms
  • 3 cups chopped bok choy
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Whisk miso, rice vinegar, honey, and 1 tsp sesame oil.
  • Brush over cod and let sit 10 minutes.
  • Sauté mushrooms in remaining sesame oil 3-4 minutes; add bok choy.
  • Push veggies aside and add cod to the skillet; cover and cook 6-8 minutes.
  • Season with pepper and serve over the sautéed veggies.
