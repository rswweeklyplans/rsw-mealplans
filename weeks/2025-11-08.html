Focus: Mineral Replenish

This week highlights potassium-, magnesium-, and zinc-rich foods. Expect potatoes, beans, leafy greens, avocado, pumpkin seeds, and broths.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 Tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • 2 Tbsp raisins
  • 1 Tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 1 tsp blackstrap molasses
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Bring water and sea salt to a boil in a saucepan.
  • Stir in oats and simmer 5 minutes until creamy.
  • Stir in milk, cinnamon, flaxseed, raisins, and molasses.
  • Top with pumpkin seeds and serve warm.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 cup frozen mango
  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds

Prep Notes / Recipe

  • Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • Pour into a glass and serve chilled.

Ingredients
  • 1.5 lb salmon fillets

  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 Tbsp sesame seeds
  • 3/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup dry quinoa
  • 2 cups water
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Rinse quinoa, add to a pot with water, bring to a boil, then simmer 15 minutes.
  • Place salmon on a sheet pan; brush with olive oil and garlic and top with lemon.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chopped Swiss chard

  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tsp chopped rosemary
  • 3/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Microwave potatoes 5–6 minutes until just tender.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 3/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
