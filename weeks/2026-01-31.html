Focus: Metabolic Flexibility

This week alternates slow-carb plates with higher-fat, lower-carb options while keeping protein steady. The variety helps your body switch fuels smoothly without energy dips.

Ingredients
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup sliced avocado
  • 1 Tbsp crumbled feta
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat olive oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  • Sauté onion and spinach for 2 minutes until wilted.
  • Whisk eggs with salt and pepper and pour over veggies.
  • Cook until set, fold in feta, and slide to a plate.
  • Top with sliced avocado and serve.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup snap peas
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper strips
  • 1 tsp avocado oil
  • 2 Tbsp tamari
  • 1/2 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1 tsp grated ginger

Ingredients
  • 6 oz salmon fillet
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup sweet potato cubes
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Preheat oven to 425°F.
  • Toss broccoli and sweet potato with half the olive oil, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  • Spread veggies on a sheet pan and roast 15 minutes.
  • Push veggies aside, add salmon, drizzle remaining oil, and season.
  • Roast 10 minutes more until salmon flakes and serve.
