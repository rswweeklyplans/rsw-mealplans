Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup banana slices
  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 2 Tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • 1 Tbsp almond butter
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Bring water and milk to a simmer in a small pot.
  • Stir in oats and sea salt; cook 5–7 minutes until creamy.
  • Remove from heat and stir in cinnamon.
  • Top with banana, chia, pumpkin seeds, and almond butter.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb ground turkey

  • 1/4 cup almond meal
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup quick-cook polenta
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 Tbsp grated Parmesan
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Mix turkey, spinach, egg, almond meal, garlic, salt, and pepper; form meatballs.

  • Stir Parmesan into polenta.
  • Serve meatballs and sauce over polenta.
