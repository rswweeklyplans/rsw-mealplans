Week start (ISO): 2025-12-27

Focus: Blood Sugar Balance

This week focuses on balanced plates: protein + fiber + healthy fats with slow-digesting carbs for steady energy. Meals favor berries, beans, oats, quinoa, and veggies while keeping added sugars low.

Ingredients
  • 3/4 cup liquid whole eggs
  • 1/4 cup liquid egg whites
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 cup baby spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1 oz feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt

Prep Notes / Recipe

  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
