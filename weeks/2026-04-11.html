Focus: Metabolic Flexibility

This week alternates slow-carb plates with higher-fat, lower-carb options while keeping protein steady. The variety helps your body switch fuels smoothly without energy dips.

Ingredients
  • 6 oz salmon fillet

  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp lemon zest
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat oven to 400°F and line a sheet pan.
  • Toss asparagus with olive oil and a pinch of salt and spread on pan.
  • Place salmon on pan, add lemon zest, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

  • Plate with warmed quinoa and serve.

Ingredients
  • 8 oz grass-fed sirloin steak
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 8 oz sweet potato, cubed
  • 3 cups chopped kale
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Roast sweet potato at 425°F with 1 tsp olive oil and a pinch of salt for 18–22 minutes.
  • Pat steak dry, season with salt and pepper, and sear in 1 tsp olive oil 3–4 minutes per side.
  • Rest steak 5 minutes and slice.
  • Sauté kale with garlic and a splash of water for 3 minutes.
  • Plate steak with sweet potato and kale.
