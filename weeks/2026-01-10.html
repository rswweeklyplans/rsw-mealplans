Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Bring almond milk and salt to a simmer in a small pot.
  • Stir in oats and cook 5–7 minutes until creamy.
  • Fold in blueberries, chia, and cinnamon.
  • Drizzle maple syrup and serve warm.

  • Stir in spinach and nutritional yeast until wilted.

Ingredients
  • 2 cups 1-inch sweet potato cubes
  • 1 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped kale
  • 1 cup cooked black beans, rinsed
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 2 Tbsp lime juice
  • 1 Tbsp chopped cilantro
Prep Notes / Recipe

  • Combine potatoes, kale, black beans, onion, and lime juice; top with cilantro.

Ingredients
  • 6 oz wild salmon fillet
  • 3 cups cauliflower florets
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat oven to 425°F and line a sheet pan.
  • Toss cauliflower with 1 Tbsp oil, half the turmeric, salt, and pepper; roast 15 minutes.
  • Push cauliflower aside; place salmon on pan and drizzle 1/2 Tbsp oil, remaining turmeric, garlic powder, and lemon juice.
  • Roast 8–10 minutes until salmon flakes; serve with cauliflower.
