Focus: Recovery & Strength

This week's meals are built around Recovery & Strength. Each plate is designed to be whole-food, home-cook-friendly, and easy to prepare in 40 minutes or less.

Ingredients
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 medium sweet potato, diced
  • 1/2 cup black beans, drained
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 Tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  • Add diced sweet potato and cook for 12-15 minutes until tender.
  • Add black beans, cumin, and paprika, stir to combine.
  • Create four wells and crack eggs into each.
  • Cover and cook for 5 minutes until eggs are set.
  • Garnish with cilantro and serve immediately.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries

Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Add Greek yogurt to a large bowl.

  • Arrange berries and banana slices on top.
  • Sprinkle with chopped walnuts.
  • Drizzle honey over everything and serve.

Ingredients
  • 4 oz smoked salmon
  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Toast bread until golden brown.
  • Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  • Poach eggs in simmering water for 3 minutes.
  • Spread avocado on toast and top with salmon.
  • Place poached eggs on top and sprinkle with red pepper flakes.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup oat flour
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 2 Tbsp butter
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Blend cottage cheese, eggs, oat flour, and vanilla until smooth.
  • Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  • Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake, cook until bubbles form.
  • Flip and cook another 2 minutes.
  • Serve topped with strawberries and maple syrup.

Ingredients
  • 8 oz turkey sausage
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Cook turkey sausage in olive oil until browned, about 8 minutes.
  • Add bell peppers and cook for 3 minutes.
  • Add spinach and stir until wilted.
  • Whisk eggs and pour into skillet, scramble until cooked.
  • Top with feta cheese and serve.

Ingredients

Ingredients

Ingredients
  • 2 ribeye steaks (8 oz each)
  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Boil sweet potatoes until tender, about 15 minutes.
  • Mash with butter and milk, season with salt.
  • Heat olive oil in a cast iron skillet over high heat.
  • Season steaks and sear 4 minutes per side for medium.
  • Add rosemary to pan, baste steaks with butter.
  • Rest steaks 5 minutes before serving with mashed sweet potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 Tbsp fresh dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Preheat oven to 400°F.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 medium zucchinis, spiralized
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Mix turkey, almond flour, egg, and garlic, form into meatballs.
  • Heat olive oil and brown meatballs on all sides, about 10 minutes.
  • Add marinara sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  • Sauté zucchini noodles in a separate pan for 3 minutes.
  • Serve meatballs and sauce over zucchini noodles with basil.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb pork tenderloin
  • 1 large head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 Tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Preheat oven to 425°F.
  • Season pork with paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  • Toss cauliflower with olive oil and spread on baking sheet.
  • Sear pork in a hot skillet for 3 minutes per side.
  • Transfer pork to oven with cauliflower, roast 20-25 minutes.
  • Slice pork and serve with cauliflower, garnish with parsley.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp red curry paste
  • 1 cup bell peppers, sliced

  • 2 Tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 cup cooked jasmine rice

Prep Notes / Recipe
