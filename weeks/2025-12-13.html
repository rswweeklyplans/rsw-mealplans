Focus: Simplify & Prep

Ingredients
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup
  • 2 Tbsp sliced almonds
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Mix oats, almond milk, chia seeds, and maple syrup in a jar.
  • Stir in half the blueberries.
  • Refrigerate 6–8 hours or overnight.
  • Top with remaining blueberries and almonds to serve.

Ingredients
  • 8 eggs
  • 1 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat oven to 350°F and brush a muffin tin with olive oil.
  • Sauté onion and bell pepper 3–4 minutes.
  • Whisk eggs with salt and pepper, then stir in veggies and spinach.
  • Divide into 8 cups and bake 18–20 minutes; cool and refrigerate.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 3/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 Tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 1 tsp honey
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Add almond milk and yogurt to a blender.
  • Add spinach, berries, flaxseed, and honey.

  • Serve immediately or chill up to 1 day.

Ingredients

Prep Notes / Recipe
