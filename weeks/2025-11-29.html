Focus: Micronutrient Density

This week packs 3+ colors per plate with herbs for extra phytonutrients. Plenty of peppers, tomatoes, berries, and greens deliver vitamins and minerals.

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup steel-cut oats
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 2 Tbsp chopped walnuts
  • 1 Tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Bring water to a boil and add oats.
  • Simmer 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Stir in almond milk, cinnamon, and salt.
  • Top with blueberries, walnuts, and flaxseed.

Ingredients
  • 2 cups chopped kale
  • 1 cup frozen pineapple
  • 1 cup sliced kiwi
  • 1/2 cup banana slices
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened kefir
  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 2 Tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • 1 Tbsp lime juice
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Blend kale, pineapple, banana, kefir, and lime juice until thick.
  • Pour into a bowl.
  • Top with kiwi, chia seeds, and pumpkin seeds.
  • Chill 5 minutes and serve.

Ingredients
  • 1 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup 1/2-inch diced sweet potato

  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Sauté sweet potato in oil 8–10 minutes until tender.
  • Add onion and bell pepper and cook 3 minutes.
  • Add spinach and wilt.
  • Beat eggs with turmeric, salt, and pepper and pour in.
  • Scramble 2–3 minutes and serve.

Ingredients
  • 12 oz wild salmon fillets
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup tri-color quinoa, dry
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Preheat oven to 425°F.
  • Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, zest, salt, and pepper.
  • Toss broccoli with half the dressing and roast 15 minutes.
  • Place salmon on sheet, brush with remaining dressing, and roast 8–10 minutes.
  • Simmer quinoa in water 15 minutes and fluff.
  • Plate quinoa, broccoli, and salmon.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup brown rice, dry
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled
  • 4 cups chopped bok choy
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp tamari
  • 1 Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 Tbsp lime juice
  • 1 Tbsp sesame seeds
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Cook brown rice in water 35 minutes.
  • Whisk tamari, rice vinegar, ginger, garlic, and lime.
  • Sauté shrimp in olive oil 3 minutes and set aside.
  • Stir-fry bok choy, carrots, and snap peas in sesame oil 4–5 minutes.
  • Return shrimp, add sauce, and cook 1–2 minutes.
  • Serve over rice and sprinkle sesame seeds.

Ingredients
  • 1.5 lb bone-in chicken thighs
  • 4 cups halved Brussels sprouts
  • 3 cups 1-inch sweet potato cubes
  • 2 1/2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp chopped rosemary
  • 1 tsp chopped thyme
  • 3/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 4 lemon wedges
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Preheat oven to 425°F.
  • Toss Brussels and sweet potato with 1 1/2 Tbsp oil, salt, and pepper.
  • Mix remaining oil with garlic, rosemary, and thyme and rub on chicken.
  • Arrange chicken and vegetables on a sheet pan.
  • Roast 25–30 minutes until chicken reaches 165°F.
  • Serve with lemon wedges.

Ingredients
  • 2 lb mussels, cleaned

  • 3 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 cups crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1 cup low-sodium broth
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes

  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 8 oz sourdough bread, sliced
  • 4 lemon wedges
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat oil and sauté garlic 1 minute.
  • Add tomatoes, wine, broth, and red pepper flakes and simmer 5 minutes.

  • Stir in chard to wilt and season.
  • Toast sourdough.
  • Serve mussels and broth with bread and lemon.
