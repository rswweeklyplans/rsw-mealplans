Ingredients
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 2 Tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 1 Tbsp almond butter
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Bring water to a boil in a small pot.
  • Stir in oats and reduce heat to low; simmer 5–7 minutes.
  • Stir in flaxseed, cinnamon, and half the blueberries.
  • Cook 1 minute more until thick and creamy.
  • Top with almond butter, remaining berries, and maple syrup.
