Ingredients
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 3/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Whisk milk, yogurt, honey, and vanilla in a jar.
  • Stir in oats, chia, and cinnamon.
  • Top with berries and press gently.
  • Cover and refrigerate at least 6 hours.
  • Stir and serve chilled.

Ingredients
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 cup diced sweet potato
  • 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup egg whites
  • 1/2 cup beaten eggs
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat oil in a skillet over medium and add sweet potato.
  • Cook 8–10 minutes until tender, then add onion and pepper.
  • Stir in spinach to wilt.
  • Pour in egg whites and beaten eggs and scramble 3–4 minutes.
  • Season with salt and pepper and serve.

Prep Notes / Recipe

Prep Notes / Recipe
