Focus: Anti-Inflammatory

This week leans on omega-3s, colorful produce, and antioxidant herbs/spices. Simple methods and olive oil keep flavors bright while minimizing fuss.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 1/4 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 3/4 cup blueberries
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp pure maple syrup
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Whisk almond milk, turmeric, ginger, vanilla, maple syrup, and salt in a jar.
  • Stir in oats, chia seeds, and half the blueberries.
  • Cover and refrigerate at least 4 hours or overnight.
  • Top with remaining blueberries and serve.

Ingredients
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced cremini mushrooms
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 Tbsp water
  • 1/4 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 medium avocado, sliced
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat oil in a skillet over medium and sauté mushrooms 3 minutes.
  • Add spinach and cook until wilted.
  • Whisk eggs with water, turmeric, salt, and pepper, then pour into skillet.
  • Scramble gently until set and top with avocado.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup buckwheat flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 Tbsp avocado oil, melted
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 tsp avocado oil for skillet
  • 2 tsp pure maple syrup
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Whisk buckwheat flour, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon.
  • Whisk almond milk, egg, oil, and vanilla, then combine with dry ingredients.
  • Fold in blueberries.
  • Heat a lightly oiled skillet over medium and pour 1/4-cup scoops.
  • Cook 2–3 minutes per side until set; serve with a light maple drizzle.

Ingredients
  • 1 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup diced yellow onion
  • 1 cup diced carrot
  • 1 Tbsp minced fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 cup dried red lentils, rinsed
  • 4 cups low-sodium vegetable broth
  • 2 cups chopped kale
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat oil in a pot and sauté onion, carrot, ginger, and garlic 3–4 minutes.
  • Stir in turmeric and lentils.
  • Add broth and simmer 15–20 minutes until lentils are tender.
  • Stir in kale to wilt 2 minutes.
  • Season with salt, pepper, and finish with lemon juice.

Ingredients
  • 2 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 1 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup dry quinoa
  • 2 cups low-sodium vegetable broth
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Preheat oven to 400°F; line a sheet pan.
  • Rub salmon with half the oil, turmeric, cumin, salt, and pepper; toss broccoli with remaining oil.
  • Roast salmon and broccoli 12–15 minutes until flaky and tender.
  • Rinse quinoa and simmer in broth 15 minutes; cover 5 minutes off heat.
  • Serve salmon and broccoli over quinoa with lemon wedges.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 Tbsp avocado oil
  • 1 Tbsp grated fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell pepper, snow peas)
  • 2 Tbsp low-sodium tamari
  • 1 Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 2 cups cooked brown rice
  • 1 Tbsp sesame seeds
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat avocado oil in a skillet; sauté ginger and garlic 30 seconds.
  • Add shrimp and cook 2–3 minutes until pink; remove.
  • Stir-fry vegetables 4–5 minutes until crisp-tender.
  • Add tamari, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and shrimp; toss to combine.
  • Serve over brown rice and sprinkle sesame seeds and red pepper flakes.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp dried dill
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 lb asparagus, trimmed
  • 1 cup wild rice blend, dry
  • 2 1/4 cups low-sodium vegetable broth
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Simmer wild rice blend in broth 35–40 minutes until tender; keep warm.
  • Preheat oven to 400°F; line a sheet pan.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb ground turkey (93% lean)
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 cups no-sugar-added marinara sauce
  • 4 cups spiralized zucchini noodles
  • 2 Tbsp chopped fresh basil
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Mix turkey, almond flour, egg, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper; form 12 meatballs.
  • Sear meatballs in olive oil 6–8 minutes, turning to brown.
  • Pour in marinara and simmer 10 minutes until cooked through.
  • Sauté zucchini noodles 2–3 minutes and serve topped with meatballs and basil.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup dry brown basmati rice

  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1 Tbsp grated fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 Tbsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can (14 oz) light coconut milk
  • 3 cups baby spinach
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Cook rice in water 25–30 minutes; cover to steam 5 minutes.

  • Add curry powder, turmeric, chickpeas, and coconut milk; simmer 10 minutes.
  • Stir in spinach to wilt, season, add lime juice, and serve over rice.
