  • 2 Tbsp chia seeds

  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon

Ingredients

  • 8 oz sweet potato, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt

Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Cook turkey sausage in a skillet until browned; remove and set aside.

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup dry green lentils
  • 1/3 cup dry quinoa
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp tahini
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp water
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Simmer lentils in water until tender, 20–25 minutes; drain.
  • Cook quinoa in 2/3 cup water 12–15 minutes; fluff.

  • Whisk tahini, lemon juice, water, and garlic powder.
  • Assemble bowl with quinoa, lentils, broccoli, and drizzle tahini.

  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp honey
