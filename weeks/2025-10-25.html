Week start (ISO): 2025-10-25

Focus: Gut Health

This week supports your gut with prebiotic fibers and gentle cooking methods. Soups, stews, and roasted veggies feature garlic, leeks, onions, asparagus, and oats.

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 3/4 cup plain kefir
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 Tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Mix oats, kefir, chia, and flaxseed in a jar.
  • Stir in honey and cinnamon.
  • Fold in blueberries.
  • Cover and chill at least 6 hours.
  • Stir and serve cold.

Ingredients
  • 2 oz sourdough bread
  • 1/2 cup mashed avocado
  • 1/2 cup beaten eggs
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup sauerkraut
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium.
  • Pour in beaten eggs and scramble 3–4 minutes.
  • Toast sourdough until crisp.
  • Mix avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  • Spread avocado on toast; top with eggs and sauerkraut.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1/2 cup frozen green banana slices
  • 3/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup cucumber chunks
  • 1 Tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1/2 cup water
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Add water, yogurt, and spinach to a blender.
  • Add banana, cucumber, flaxseed, and ginger.
  • Blend until smooth, about 45 seconds.
  • Pour into a glass and serve.

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup buckwheat groats
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 3/4 cup apple slices
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • 1 Tbsp chopped walnuts
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Simmer buckwheat with water for 10 minutes.
  • Stir in almond milk and cook 5 minutes more.
  • Warm apple slices with cinnamon in a pan 4 minutes.
  • Spoon porridge into a bowl and top with apples.
  • Drizzle maple syrup and sprinkle walnuts.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped zucchini
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup baby spinach
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Pat tofu dry and crumble.

Ingredients
  • 3/4 cup dry green lentils
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Rinse lentils under cool water.
  • Sauté onion, carrots, and celery in olive oil 5 minutes.
  • Add garlic, thyme, lentils, and broth.
  • Simmer until lentils are tender, about 25 minutes.
  • Stir in spinach and salt; cook 2 minutes.
  • Serve hot.

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup dry quinoa
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup sweet potato cubes
  • 1 cup canned chickpeas, rinsed
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 cups arugula
  • 2 Tbsp tahini
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp water
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Cook quinoa with water until fluffy, about 15 minutes.
  • Toss sweet potato with 1/2 Tbsp oil and smoked paprika; roast at 425°F for 20 minutes.
  • Warm chickpeas with 1/2 Tbsp oil in a skillet 3 minutes.
  • Whisk tahini, lemon juice, and 2 Tbsp water.
  • Assemble arugula, quinoa, potatoes, and chickpeas; drizzle dressing.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced leeks
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 2 cups diced potatoes
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
Prep Notes / Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp mirin

  • 1 tsp sesame oil

Prep Notes / Recipe
