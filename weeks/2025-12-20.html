Focus: Protein Prioritization

This week emphasizes steady, satisfying protein at each meal to support energy, recovery, and craving control. We pair lean proteins with fiber-rich sides and simple sauces so prep stays quick and repeatable.

Ingredients
  • 2 large eggs
  • 4 oz liquid egg whites
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1 oz feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Warm oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  • Add spinach and sauté 1–2 minutes until wilted.
  • Whisk eggs, egg whites, salt, and pepper; pour into skillet.
  • Stir until just set and top with feta.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning

  • 1.5 cups marinara sauce (no sugar added)

Prep Notes / Recipe
