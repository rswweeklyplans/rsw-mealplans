Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats

  • 1 Tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
Prep Notes / Recipe

  • 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Preheat oven to 400°F and line a sheet pan.

Ingredients
  • 8 oz soba noodles

  • 1 Tbsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1 Tbsp minced garlic
  • 6 cups low-sodium vegetable broth
  • 2 Tbsp white miso paste
  • 1 Tbsp tamari
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup sliced green onions
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Cook soba noodles per package; rinse and drain.
  • Sauté mushrooms in avocado oil 5 minutes.

Ingredients
  • 1 spaghetti squash (3 lb)
  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 24 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Preheat oven to 400°F; halve squash, remove seeds, brush with 1 tsp olive oil, and roast cut-side down 35–40 minutes.
  • Mix turkey, almond flour, egg, garlic powder, oregano, salt, and pepper; form 1-inch balls.
  • Heat 1 Tbsp olive oil in a skillet; brown meatballs 3–4 minutes.
  • Add crushed tomatoes and red pepper flakes; simmer 12–15 minutes until meatballs are cooked.
  • Scrape squash into strands and top with meatballs, sauce, and basil.
