Focus: Simplify & Prep

This week's meals are built around Simplify & Prep. Each plate is designed to be whole-food, home-cook-friendly, and easy to prepare in 40 minutes or less.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Combine oats, milk, yogurt, and honey in a jar.
  • Stir in chia seeds.
  • Refrigerate overnight.
  • Top with berries before serving.

Ingredients
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Preheat oven to 350°F.
  • Whisk eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
  • Divide spinach and bell pepper into 12 greased muffin cups.
  • Pour egg mixture over vegetables and bake 20 minutes.

Ingredients
  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Toast bread until golden.
  • Mash avocado with salt, pepper flakes, and lemon juice.
  • Spread avocado mixture on toast.
  • Serve immediately.

Ingredients
  • 2 frozen bananas

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Add all ingredients to blender.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Pour into glass and serve.

Ingredients
  • 1.5 cups plain Greek yogurt

Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Layer yogurt in a bowl or jar.

Ingredients
  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup diced mango
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Whisk chia seeds, coconut milk, maple syrup, and vanilla.
  • Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  • Stir and top with mango before serving.

Ingredients
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 2 Tbsp chopped fresh chives
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Whisk eggs with salt and pepper.
  • Melt butter in skillet over medium-low heat.
  • Add eggs and stir gently until soft curds form.
  • Remove from heat, fold in chives, and serve.

Ingredients
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

  • 1 cup diced cucumber
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes halved
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onion
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese cubed
  • 1/2 cup kalamata olives
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Layer dressing at bottom of jar.

  • Seal and refrigerate up to 4 days.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise

  • 1/4 cup diced celery
  • 2 Tbsp chopped fresh dill

Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Mix chicken, mayonnaise, mustard, celery, and dill.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup dried brown lentils
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Sauté onion, carrots, celery, and garlic in pot with 1 Tbsp olive oil.
  • Add lentils, broth, and cumin.
  • Bring to boil then simmer 25 minutes until lentils are tender.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Ingredients

Prep Notes / Recipe

Ingredients
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans drained
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 Tbsp lime juice
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Combine beans, corn, bell pepper, and cilantro.

Ingredients
  • 4 chicken thighs bone-in
  • 1 lb baby potatoes halved
  • 2 cups green beans trimmed
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Preheat oven to 425°F.
  • Toss potatoes and green beans with 2 Tbsp olive oil on sheet pan.
  • Season chicken with remaining oil, lemon juice, oregano, and salt.
  • Nestle chicken among vegetables and roast 35 minutes.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb beef stew meat cubed
  • 3 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups diced potatoes
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp salt
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Brown beef in Dutch oven with 1 Tbsp oil.
  • Add broth, potatoes, carrots, celery, thyme, and salt.
  • Bring to boil then reduce to simmer.
  • Cook covered for 30 minutes until vegetables are tender.

Ingredients
  • 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 1 lb asparagus trimmed
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 lemon sliced
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Preheat oven to 400°F.
  • Arrange asparagus on sheet pan and drizzle with 1 Tbsp oil.
  • Place salmon on pan, top with garlic, lemon slices, remaining oil, salt, and pepper.
  • Bake 15 minutes until salmon flakes easily.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb large shrimp peeled and deveined
  • 4 medium zucchini spiralized
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 2 Tbsp fresh parsley chopped
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat.
  • Sauté garlic 30 seconds then add shrimp.
  • Cook shrimp 3 minutes per side, add wine and simmer 2 minutes.
  • Toss in zucchini noodles, cook 2 minutes, and garnish with parsley.

Prep Notes / Recipe
