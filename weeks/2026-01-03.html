Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 Tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 3/4 cup plain kefir
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1 tsp honey
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Combine oats, chia, and flax in a jar.
  • Pour in kefir and stir well.
  • Cover and refrigerate at least 6 hours.
  • Top with berries and honey to serve.

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup low-sodium vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp white miso paste
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 2 Tbsp sliced scallions
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Simmer oats in broth 5–7 minutes until creamy.
  • Soft-boil egg 6 minutes, cool, and peel.
  • Stir miso into oats off heat.
  • Top with egg, sesame oil, scallions, and sesame seeds.

Ingredients
  • 3/4 cup buckwheat flour

  • 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 medium apple, diced
  • 1 tsp coconut oil
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Whisk flour, chia, and baking powder; add almond milk and vanilla.
  • Heat skillet with 1/2 tsp coconut oil; pour 1/4 cup batter rounds.
  • Cook 2–3 minutes per side; repeat with remaining batter.
  • Sauté apples with 1/2 tsp coconut oil and cinnamon 3–4 minutes.
  • Serve pancakes topped with warm apples and maple syrup.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 3 cups chopped baby bok choy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sliced bell peppers
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 cups cooked brown rice
  • 1/2 cup kimchi
Prep Notes / Recipe

  • Serve over rice with kimchi on the side.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
