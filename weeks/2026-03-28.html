Focus: Mineral Replenish

This week highlights potassium-, magnesium-, and zinc-rich foods. Expect potatoes, beans, leafy greens, avocado, pumpkin seeds, and broths.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup unsweetened kefir
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 1 cup frozen banana slices
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 Tbsp almond butter
  • 2 Tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 Tbsp cacao nibs
  • 1/2 cup sliced strawberries
  • 1/4 cup mineral-rich granola
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Blend kefir, spinach, banana, water, and almond butter until smooth.
  • Pour into a bowl.
  • Top with pumpkin seeds, chia seeds, and cacao nibs.
  • Add strawberries and granola, then serve.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 Tbsp low-sodium tamari
  • 1 Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp arrowroot starch
  • 1 Tbsp water
  • 1 cup cooked millet
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat oil in a skillet and sear beef 2–3 minutes; remove.
  • Stir-fry broccoli, garlic, and ginger 4 minutes.
