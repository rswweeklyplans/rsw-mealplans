Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Stir oats, almond milk, and yogurt in a jar.
  • Mix in chia seeds and maple syrup.
  • Top with blueberries.
  • Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  • Grab and eat chilled or add a splash of milk.

Ingredients
  • 6 large eggs (10.5 oz)
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp olive oil
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Preheat oven to 350°F and oil a 6-cup muffin tin.
  • Whisk eggs with salt and pepper.
  • Fold in spinach, bell pepper, and cheddar.
  • Divide into cups and bake 18–20 minutes.
  • Cool 5 minutes and release from tin.

Prep Notes / Recipe

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp minced garlic

Ingredients
  • 1 lb lean ground turkey
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (15 oz) can black beans, rinsed
  • 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1/2 tsp salt
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Heat oil in a pot and brown turkey 5–6 minutes.

Ingredients
  • 12 oz salmon fillet
  • 12 oz green beans, trimmed
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Preheat oven to 400°F and line a sheet pan.
  • Toss green beans with 1 Tbsp oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  • Place salmon on pan; brush with remaining oil, lemon zest, and juice.
  • Bake 12–15 minutes until salmon flakes and beans are tender.
  • Rest 3 minutes and serve.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups snap peas
  • 1 cup sliced bell pepper
  • 1 Tbsp avocado oil
  • 2 Tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 2 Tbsp water
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp minced ginger
  • 2 cups cooked jasmine rice
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Whisk soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, cornstarch, and water.
  • Heat avocado oil in a large skillet over high.
  • Stir-fry shrimp 2–3 minutes until pink; remove.
  • Stir-fry snap peas, bell pepper, garlic, and ginger 3–4 minutes.
  • Return shrimp, pour sauce, and toss 1 minute to thicken.
  • Serve over warm rice.

Ingredients
  • 2 cups diced sweet potato (about 1 lb)
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 (15 oz) can black beans, rinsed
  • 6 oz corn tortillas
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 oz queso fresco, crumbled (1/2 cup)
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Preheat oven to 425°F and line a sheet pan.
  • Toss sweet potatoes with oil, chili powder, cumin, and salt.
  • Roast 20–25 minutes until tender and browned.
  • Warm black beans and tortillas.
  • Fill tortillas with potatoes and beans; top with salsa, cilantro, and queso.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 12 oz zucchini noodles
Prep Notes / Recipe
  • Mix turkey, breadcrumbs, egg, seasoning, garlic, and salt; form 12 meatballs.

  • Toss in zucchini noodles and cook 2–3 minutes.
  • Serve immediately.
